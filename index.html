FIRST MERGED GROUP REPORTING

In our group's presentation on the characteristics of a 21st century assessment, we delved into the roles of assessments plays during classroom sessions. We examined how assessments serve not only as measures of student's performance but also as an invaluable tool for guiding instruction. During the discussion, we discovered the importance of aligning assessments with capabilities of the students to ensure that they effectively evaluate and absorb the lesson. Moreover, we explored various assessment methods, including formative, summative, and authentic assessments, highlighting their unique contributions to the over-all learning process. In conclusion, we were able to emphasize the critical need for educators to thoughtfully integrate assessments into the teaching practices to promote meaningful learning outcomes and student success.
